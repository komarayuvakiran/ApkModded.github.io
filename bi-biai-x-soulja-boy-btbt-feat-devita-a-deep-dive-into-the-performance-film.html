B.I (비아이) X Soulja Boy 'BTBT' (Feat. DeVita) – A Deep Dive into the Performance Film

max May 31, 2025

 

When two worlds collide, the result can be electrifying. The collaboration between B.I (비아이), the Korean hip-hop sensation, and American rapper Soulja Boy, featuring the soulful vocals of DeVita, is exactly that—a fusion of styles, cultures, and artistry that resonates deeply with fans worldwide. The performance film for 'BTBT', produced by 131online and AITTY TOO, showcases this dynamic collaboration in a vibrant, kinetic visual and musical experience that’s impossible to ignore.

The Artists Behind 'BTBT'

B.I, formerly leader of the iconic K-pop group iKON, has carved out a solo career marked by emotional depth, innovative sounds, and compelling storytelling. His collaboration with Soulja Boy, a pioneer in viral hip-hop hits in the US, marks a cross-cultural exchange that blends Korean and American hip-hop influences seamlessly.

Adding another layer to this collaboration is DeVita, whose powerful vocals provide a haunting and melodic contrast to the energetic verses. The synergy between these three artists elevates 'BTBT' beyond a simple track, turning it into a memorable musical journey.

The Essence of 'BTBT': Lyrics and Themes

The lyrics of 'BTBT' capture a blend of swagger, introspection, and raw emotion. Lines like "side to side pull up then I drop the top on a new ride" paint vivid images of success and celebration, while "every time I drop a new hit we on fire, yeah we going viral" reflects the artists' confidence and awareness of their growing influence.

However, beneath the flashy exterior lies a deeper message about perseverance and rising from humble beginnings. The phrase "y'all came from the ground" speaks to the journey from obscurity to fame, a theme that resonates universally with anyone chasing their dreams.

The song's rhythm flows with a contagious energy, punctuated by Soulja Boy's signature style and B.I's melodic rap verses. This blend creates a track that is both club-ready and introspective, appealing to a broad audience.

Visual Storytelling Through Performance

The official performance film directed by 131online and choreographed by AITTY TOO (@82_aittytoo) is a masterclass in visual storytelling. The choreography complements the song’s rhythm and mood perfectly, combining sharp, precise movements with fluid transitions that mirror the song’s dynamic beats.

The visual elements emphasize the artists' charisma and the song’s themes. The imagery of "jumped off the stage I don't even know why" conveys the spontaneous energy and passion that fuel their performances. The performance film doesn't just showcase the music; it brings the story and emotions of 'BTBT' to life.

Choreography by AITTY TOO

AITTY TOO’s choreography is a standout feature of the performance film. It blends street-style hip-hop with contemporary dance, creating a visually compelling narrative that enhances the song’s impact. The dancers' movements echo the lyrics, such as the "side to side" motion that visually represents the rhythm and flow described in the song.

This choreography elevates the performance, making it not just a musical experience but a full-bodied artistic expression. It invites viewers to feel the music physically, bridging the gap between audio and visual storytelling.

The Cultural Impact and Viral Success

Since its release, 'BTBT' has become more than just a song—it’s a cultural phenomenon. The collaboration between B.I and Soulja Boy, featuring DeVita, has sparked conversations about the globalization of music and the blending of genres across borders.

The lyric "yeah we going viral, I know that I don't surprise you" is prophetic, highlighting the artists' understanding of the digital age's power in music dissemination. Platforms like YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram have played a crucial role in propelling 'BTBT' to viral status, with fans worldwide sharing their own renditions of the choreography and engaging with the song’s vibrant energy.

Global Reach and Fan Engagement

B.I’s official social media channels, including his YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok, along with 131online’s presence on Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook, have been instrumental in connecting with fans and amplifying the song’s reach. Their active engagement fosters a sense of community among listeners, encouraging them to participate in the song’s ongoing narrative.

This global interaction underscores the song’s universal appeal. Despite language differences, the infectious beat, compelling visuals, and heartfelt performances transcend cultural barriers, making 'BTBT' a shared experience across continents.

Production Quality and Artistic Direction

The production quality of 'BTBT' is impeccable. Every element, from the beat to the vocals to the visual effects, is carefully crafted to create an immersive experience. The synergy between the artists’ voices and the instrumental track creates a layered soundscape that is both catchy and emotionally resonant.

131online’s artistic direction shines through in the performance film’s sleek cinematography and editing. The use of lighting, camera angles, and color palettes enhances the mood and energy of the song. These visual techniques draw viewers deeper into the narrative, making the performance not just a display of talent but a cinematic event.

Behind the Scenes: Collaboration and Creativity

The collaboration between B.I, Soulja Boy, DeVita, and the production team illustrates the power of creative partnerships. Each artist brings their unique style and strengths, resulting in a track that is fresh, innovative, and compelling.

The choreography by AITTY TOO, combined with 131online’s visionary production, demonstrates how music and dance can unite to tell a powerful story. This synergy is a testament to the importance of multidisciplinary collaboration in today’s music industry.

Why 'BTBT' Resonates with Audiences

At its core, 'BTBT' resonates because it captures the spirit of ambition, resilience, and celebration. The song’s infectious energy invites listeners to join in the moment, whether through dance, singing along, or simply feeling the vibe.

Its themes of rising from the ground and going viral echo the dreams of countless artists and fans alike. The lyrics and performance celebrate success while acknowledging the hard work and passion behind it.

Moreover, the fusion of Korean and American hip-hop elements broadens its appeal, making it a bridge between different musical worlds. This inclusivity and diversity are part of what makes 'BTBT' stand out in a crowded music landscape.

Conclusion: A Must-Experience Performance

'BTBT' by B.I X Soulja Boy featuring DeVita is more than just a song—it's an experience. Through its captivating lyrics, powerful choreography, and high-quality production, it captures the essence of modern hip-hop while paying homage to the artists’ roots and ambitions.

This performance film is a shining example of how music can transcend language and culture, bringing people together through shared emotion and artistry. Whether you’re a fan of K-pop, hip-hop, or simply great music, 'BTBT' offers something unforgettable.

For those who haven’t yet experienced the performance film, it’s well worth the watch—and listen. Dive into the world of B.I, Soulja Boy, and DeVita, and feel the energy that has made 'BTBT' a viral sensation across the globe.
