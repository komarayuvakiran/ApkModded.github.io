YOARI’s “TRUE” from the My Demon OST: Lyrics, Meaning, and Drama Impact

max June 7, 2025

 

Korean dramas would be incomplete without their unforgettable soundtracks. These songs often capture raw emotion and tell stories in ways dialogue alone cannot. “TRUE” by YOARI is no exception. As Part 6 of the My Demon Original Television Soundtrack, this song stands out for its intense vocals and haunting melody, capturing the turmoil of the lead characters in the SBS drama “My Demon” starring Kim Yoo-jung and Song Kang. Let’s explore how the lyrics and music of “TRUE” tap into the spirit of the show and draw listeners into its emotional world.

The Power of Drama OSTs

Soundtracks set the emotional scene in drama series. In Korean dramas, these original soundtracks (OSTs) often become fan favorites. They’re not just background music. They add depth to the characters’ struggles, hopes, and dreams. With the rise of streaming and digital sharing, OSTs like “TRUE” reach global audiences fast. Fans now discover new artists through these soundtracks and add them to their playlists, much like how “TRUE” finds listeners outside the context of “My Demon.”

Curious about how Korean drama OSTs impact the viewing experience? Explore collections like the Best of Korean OSTs and read what fans think about their favorite k-drama soundtracks in this Reddit discussion on favorite k-drama OSTs.

Introducing YOARI: The Voice Behind "TRUE"

YOARI, a well-known vocalist in the Korean music scene, is praised for her emotive delivery and haunting voice. In “TRUE,” she weaves intricate feelings of pain, doubt, and longing, all in a pop song with a mysterious, cinematic touch. YOARI’s performance is more than just singing; it’s an experience that mirrors the lead character’s inner turmoil in “My Demon.”

My Demon: Drama, Conflict, and the Role of “TRUE”

“My Demon” blends fantasy, romance, and suspense, following the journey of Jeong Guwon (played by Song Kang). He’s a supernatural being torn between destroying and saving those around him. The show’s themes—redemption, choice, and inner darkness—echo throughout the plot. This is where “TRUE” becomes essential.

The song captures the moment when a character stands at a crossroads. The recurring line, “Tell me the truth, this is the end, I feel like losing myself,” reflects the uncertainty and pain that Jeong Guwon faces. As he wrestles with fate and his own nature, the song plays like an internal monologue.

Dissecting the Lyrics: What Is “TRUE” Really About?

Let’s look closer at the lyrics that make “TRUE” such a powerful addition to the show:

1. Facing the Truth

"Tell me the truth
This is the end
I feel like losing myself
Can't find my way
To not lose you
Why is it so complicated?"

The singer voices a plea for honesty and clarity. The repeated question, “Why is it so complicated?” gets at the tangled relationships and hidden motives in the drama. The desperation to hold onto someone while at risk of losing yourself mirrors key plot moments in “My Demon.”

2. The Struggle With Darkness

"Ooh-ooh, hoo-hoo
Bring me to the dark
Can't find you in the end
Don't ever cry for me"

Here, the lyrics suggest surrender to darker emotions—a recurring theme in tragic love stories and dramas featuring supernatural elements. The inability to find someone “in the end” can mean many things: permanent loss, fate, or the unknown consequences of difficult choices.

3. Choosing a Path

"This is the moment, come
Now, it's time to choose"

The song makes clear that the character must choose between two paths, each with deep consequences. This fits the critical points of the series, when decisions made by the leads affect not only themselves but everyone around them.

The Sound: How "TRUE" Sets the Mood

Music isn’t just about words—it’s about feeling. “TRUE” features a tense bass line and mysterious piano melodies, layering emotion throughout the track. The production is spacious but focused, giving YOARI’s vocals the room to soar over the arrangement. The atmosphere created by the song mirrors the haunting, supernatural world of “My Demon,” reinforcing the drama’s sense of urgency and suspense.

YOARI’s Voice: A Perfect Match For “My Demon”

Not every singer can deliver lyrics about fear, love, and loss with conviction. YOARI stands out because her voice carries a weight that matches the story’s gravity. Listening to “TRUE” without knowing the show, you still sense the struggle at its core. Fans often revisit songs like this to relive the emotional moments seen on-screen. YOARI’s vocal performance turns “TRUE” into more than just a soundtrack—it becomes a narrative all its own.

The Cultural Impact of K-Drama OSTs

Korean OSTs are more than background music. They can turn a simple scene into something heartbreaking or uplifting. “TRUE” shows why fans of “My Demon” keep returning to the soundtrack long after the episode airs. Well-composed OSTs elevate an entire drama, sometimes becoming chart-toppers or viral hits.

Other My Demon OST tracks feature artists like NewJeans, Roy Kim, Winter, and Sam Kim—each providing their own unique sound and supporting the series’ rollercoaster of emotions.

Listening to “TRUE” Beyond the Drama

If you’re one of the many fans looking for ways to enjoy “TRUE” outside the show, there are several official releases. The full single, complete with all the emotion and nuance from the original scenes, is available on music platforms such as Apple Music’s My Demon OST Part 6 and other streaming platforms.

And if “TRUE” has hooked you on Korean drama soundtracks, consider exploring playlists like the Best KDRAMA OST (Updated 2024). You might discover more songs and artists that speak to your moods long after you finish watching.

The Emotional Heartbeat of “My Demon”

Some songs are made to be background noise. “TRUE” is not one of them. It climbs into your memory, the lyrics and melody returning every time you think of the series’ most critical scenes. The heartbreak, the hope, the tough decisions—YOARI’s song doesn’t just tell a story. It invites you to feel every bit of it alongside the characters.

Conclusion

“TRUE” by YOARI is one of those rare OST tracks that lives on past the show it was made for. It delivers emotion, asks hard questions, and immerses you in the painful choices at the heart of “My Demon.” Whether you’re a drama fan or someone looking for moving music, “TRUE” will pull you in.

Songs like these remind us why Korean drama soundtracks are so beloved. They turn moments into memories, shaping the way we experience a story. Listen to YOARI’s “TRUE” whenever you need to hear the truth, face your darkness, or remember that choosing your own path is sometimes the boldest thing you can do.
